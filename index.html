Assignment Question:

Given the following information for a small business, prepare the basic financial statements:

  • Balance Sheet Information:
    • Assets: Cash $5,000, Accounts Receivable $2,000, Inventory $3,000
    • Liabilities: Accounts Payable $1,500, Loans Payable $2,500
    • Owner’s Equity: $6,000
  • Income Statement Information:
    • Revenues: Service Revenue $4,000
    • Expenses: Rent $800, Salaries $1,200, Utilities $300

Tasks:

  1. Prepare a balance sheet as of the end of the period.
  2. Prepare an income statement for the period.
  3. Explain how each statement reflects the financial position and performance of the business.

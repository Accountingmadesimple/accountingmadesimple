Assignment Question:

Assume you are the accountant for a small business that has recently started its operations. The business has recorded the following transactions during its first month:

  • Invested $50,000 in cash to start the business.
  • Purchased office supplies for $1,200 on credit.
  • Paid $800 for utilities.
  • Earned $3,000 in revenue from services provided.
  • Paid $1,500 for rent.

Tasks:

  1. Prepare a trial balance for the end of the month.
  2. Create a basic income statement and a balance sheet as of the end of the month.
  3. Identify and explain any two key accounting principles applied in preparing these financial statements.
